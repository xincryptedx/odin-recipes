Banana Bread

Description

This is a recipe for green tea. Everyone likes this beverage. If you don't like this, see a doctor immediately.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons green tea leaves
  • 2 cups water
  • Honey, to taste

Directions

  1. To begin making Green Tea Recipe, take a saucepan and start heating water on a high heat.
  2. Once the water starts boiling, turn off the heat and add the green tea leaves.
  3. Keep it covered for one minute.
  4. Strain the tea into the tea cups.
  5. Add honey to taste.
